  "details": "This document is sent by an injured party (claimant/prospective plaintiff) or their legal representative (plaintiff's law firm) to a government entity, municipality, or public authority (the prospective defendant, such as a city, county, or state agency). It is a statutory prerequisite required before filing a formal civil lawsuit against a government body. The document serves to officially notify the municipality of an impending tort claim (e.g., a trip and fall personal injury), providing them with sufficient details to investigate the incident, evaluate potential liability, and potentially settle the matter before a formal summons and complaint are filed in court."
