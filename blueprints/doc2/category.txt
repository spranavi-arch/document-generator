The recipient is usually the prospective defendant (such as a corporation, employer, union, business owner, or individual) or their corporate/general counsel. The primary purposes of this document are to formally put the prospective defendant on notice of impending legal action, summarize the factual and legal basis of the claims (e.g., sexual harassment, racial discrimination), demand a settlement or amicable resolution to avoid costly litigation, and impose a strict legal duty on the defendant to preserve any relevant evidence (spoliation hold)."
